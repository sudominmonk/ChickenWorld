Product Information

The Kadaknath or Kali Masi is an Indian breed of chicked local to Jhabua and Dhar Districts of eastern Mashya Pradesh, where it is known as "Kali Masi"(fowl having black flesh). Sue to its high protein and very low fat and cholestrol levels, it is in high demand. Its fat content, is 0.73-1.03% compared to 13 to 25% in most other chicken breeds.

We have created a great environment in out poultry for production of high quality meat from the fowl. The meat is produced from high quality breed with free range techniques in which the fowl are fed and grown in a free moving environment. Unlike the other methodology where the birds are constrained to a specific location and fed.
